Executive Summary

ZARUverse is a branded crypto ecosystem engineered by Makese Holding Ltd. It blends viral participation with luxury-grade token architecture. The ecosystem includes three core assets: MAK1, DMD, and ZARU—each with distinct roles in treasury, access, and governance.
